Extension Cord Safety

When using extension cords in the office, remember these key safety guidelines:

  • Never run cords under carpets or across walkways
  • Inspect cords regularly for damage or fraying
  • Don't overload cords with multiple devices
  • Use extension cords temporarily, not as permanent solutions
Pro Tip:

Extension cords should be rated for their intended use. Make sure to check the amperage rating before connecting devices.
